## Compliance

### What is compliance validation?
Compliance validation automatically checks your creative against retailer and brand guidelines (e.g., logo size, safe zones, required elements) to ensure it meets all requirements before going live.

### Which retailers are supported?
Currently:
- **Tesco:** 15 specific rules
- **More retailers:** Coming soon!

Can't find your retailer? Contact us to request support.

### What does the compliance score mean?
The compliance score (0-100%) indicates how well your creative meets the guidelines:
- **90-100%:** Excellent - Ready to go
- **70-89%:** Good - Minor improvements needed
- **Below 70%:** Needs work - Address critical errors

### What's the difference between errors and warnings?
- **Errors (Red):** Critical issues that must be fixed before the creative can be used
- **Warnings (Yellow):** Recommendations for improvement, but not blocking

### How does the AI Copilot help with compliance?
The AI Copilot:
- Explains why a rule exists
- Provides business context
- Suggests specific fixes
- Offers learning tips to avoid future issues

### Can I override compliance rules?
Enterprise customers can configure custom rules and override settings. Contact your account manager for details.

### How do I get a compliance certificate?
1. Run a compliance check
2. Fix all errors (warnings are okay)
3. Click "Certify"
4. Download your certificate

Certificates include a unique ID, timestamp, and cryptographic signature for verification.

## Troubleshooting

### My creative won't load
Try:
1. Refresh the page
2. Clear browser cache
3. Try a different browser
4. Check your internet connection

Still not working? Contact support.

### AI generation is slow
AI generation typically takes 5-15 seconds. If it's taking longer:
- Check your internet connection
- Try again in a few minutes
- Simplify your prompt

### Export failed
Common causes:
- Internet connection interrupted
- Too many formats selected
- Large file sizes

Try selecting fewer formats or check your connection.

### Compliance check not working
Ensure:
- Your creative has content (not blank)
- You've selected a retailer
- Your internet connection is stable

### I can't upload images
Check:
- File size < 10MB
- File format is JPG, PNG, or WebP
- You haven't reached your storage limit

## Best Practices

### How can I create better creatives?
1. **Start with AI:** Use AI layouts as a foundation
2. **Keep it simple:** Don't overcrowd your design
3. **Use brand assets:** Maintain consistency
4. **Check compliance early:** Run checks throughout design
5. **Test on platforms:** Preview how it looks on each platform

### How do I improve compliance scores?
1. **Learn the rules:** Use AI Copilot to understand guidelines
2. **Check early and often:** Don't wait until the end
3. **Use templates:** Start with compliant templates
4. **Safe zones:** Keep critical elements away from edges
5. **Logo size:** Ensure logos meet minimum requirements

### How can I work faster?
1. **Keyboard shortcuts:** Learn and use them
2. **Reuse assets:** Build a library of approved assets
3. **Templates:** Create and save templates
4. **Batch export:** Export all formats at once
5. **AI tools:** Let AI handle repetitive tasks
